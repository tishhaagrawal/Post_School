Using OFX Film Emulation Plugins

Film Convert

Best plugin to start with film emulation. Film Convert is simple yet feature rich. It's film response grain is the best feature.

Dehancer

It has the most comprehensive list of film stocks. And other analogue features like bloom, halation & film weave.

Look Designer

It is the most versatile film emulator, has subtractive color space tools. Works with ACES, DWG & all major color spaces.
